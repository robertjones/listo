Listo
About

The list-making party game

Listo is an online parlour game on your phone. Perfect for dinner parties, train journeys or family get-togethers. Compete to match as many answers as possible, no answers, or just one answer with other players.

This game was inspired by the boardgame, Listography, but the challenges and cards are original.

How to play

  1. Read: Read the category and challenge aloud and then start the 30 second timer.
  2. Write: Everyone writes down their answers.
  3. Score: When the buzzer sounds, the first player reads out their list to see if their answers match with other players, then the other players go through their remaining answers in turn. Players keep their own score.

For 3-8 players. Rotate the role of first player clockwise after every turn.
